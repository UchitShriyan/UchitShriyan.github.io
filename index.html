Projects


Generative AI-Driven Digital Twin for 3D Printing (Fall 2023 - Present)
In my Master’s Applied Project, I am pioneering generative AI for digital twin creation, optimizing 3D printing processes like Aerosol Jet Printing (AJP). This venture aims to revolutionize manufacturing in sectors including aerospace, automotive, and medical by comprehending complex 3D printing dynamics. Harnessing high-value data and advanced generative AI, the project transforms raw data into predictive manufacturing insights. It focuses on manufacturability predictions of AJP part geometries and addresses challenges in robotics and autonomous systems, like AI-driven optimization and trajectory planning, marking a significant advance in 3D printing quality and efficiency.


Global Gesture-Controlled Car Using Arduino Nano BLE 33 Sense and MQTT (Fall 2023)
In this project, I co-engineered an IoT car with intuitive gesture control using Arduino Nano 33 IoT. This involved embedding a machine learning model for gesture recognition and establishing vehicle communication via MQTT protocol and AWS EC2. Unique to this project was the remote operation feature, where the controller and car were connected to different networks in different locations, demonstrating advanced remote control capabilities over the internet. This project demonstrates a seamless blend of machine learning and IoT, showcasing innovative solutions in remote vehicle control and human-machine interaction.
